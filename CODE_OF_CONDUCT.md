# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community. We expect all participants to follow these guidelines:

- Be respectful and considerate
- Use inclusive language
- Do not harass or discriminate
- Assume good faith; seek to understand
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or sustained disruption of discussion.

Reporting:
- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers at opensource@shayyz.dev
- Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially

Enforcement:
- Maintainers may take any action deemed appropriate, including warnings or bans from community spaces

This policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant.
